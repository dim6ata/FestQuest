What Is FestQuest?

New Day Secret Events

Welcome to the land of the curious!
We love questions - they make us learn, they make us experience, they teach us about the world around us and mostly - about ourselves!
We take that idea and invite people to experience what they haven't tried before.
We provide gloriously decorated settings, entertainment like no other and a wealth of opportunities for people to share something new with other fellow adventurers.
We ask one thing of you:
'Allow yourself to be surprised'!

Fun, Yet Useful!

We love art, music, theatre and all that is beautiful in the world. We like to share this with others and urge people to do the same.
Our events offer the opportunity for audience and artists to collaborate and allow each other to participate in the experience actively.
Audience as artists and artists as audience - sharing our secrets to help each other grow.
In the spirit of collaboration - we offer a large portion of our earnings to charities, therefore we share not just inside the secret, but also with the world outside.

How Does It Work?

Join The Other Kids!

If you have the feeling these events will bring out your child-like nature - you are absolutely right!
We are happy to see people play, explore and laugh together. All that is needed is a brave first step and then the rest will be history.
Fill in the Join Us form below and we will get in touch with you shortly.
We choose our participants carefully, therefore by filling in the 'About You' section, you will help us decide whether this specific event is good for you.
The more sincere you are the more likely to be selected. See you on the other side!

Bring Joy To The World

Once you have been selected, you will receive a conformation that will allow you to book your tickets.
You will know the theme and the date of the event, but you will receive details about its location only a few hours before it takes place,
We encourage you to dress up in style that you love, bring your nice big smile and enjoy the large variety of performances, installations, great people and surroundings.
We are always happy to hear from you, so feel free to drop us a line at our Comments section.
